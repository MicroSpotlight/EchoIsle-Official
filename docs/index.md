# Echo Isle

Echo Isle, called 声屿 in Chinese, is a focused podcast player for iOS. It helps you discover shows, import public RSS feeds, subscribe to podcasts, keep playback progress, and save episodes for personal offline listening.

## What Echo Isle Does

- Search and discover podcasts.
- Import shows directly from public RSS feed URLs.
- Subscribe to shows and browse real episode lists.
- Play audio in the background with Lock Screen controls.
- Remember playback progress.
- Skip, change speed, and continue between episodes.
- Download episodes to your device for personal offline listening.
- Keep subscriptions, playback history, progress, and downloads locally on your device.

## Privacy By Default

Echo Isle does not require an account. It does not use your subscriptions, playback history, or downloads for advertising tracking.
