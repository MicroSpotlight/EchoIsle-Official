# Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-31

Echo Isle, called 声屿 in Chinese, is a podcast player for iOS. This Privacy Policy explains how Echo Isle handles data.

## Summary

Echo Isle does not require an account. The app is designed to keep your podcast subscriptions, playback progress, playback history, and downloaded episodes on your device.

Echo Isle does not use advertising SDKs and does not use your subscriptions, playback history, or downloads for cross-app or cross-site tracking.

## Data Stored On Your Device

Echo Isle may store the following data locally on your device:

- Podcast subscriptions.
- Show and episode metadata cached from RSS feeds and podcast search.
- Playback progress.
- Playback history.
- Downloaded episode audio files.
- App settings, such as appearance, language, playback speed, skip interval, cache settings, and download preferences.
- Cached cover artwork and feed responses.

You can remove downloaded episodes and clear local cache from inside the app where the relevant controls are available. You can also remove app data by deleting the app from your device.

## Network Requests

Echo Isle uses network requests to provide podcast features:

- Podcast search terms are sent to the iTunes Podcast Search API to find shows.
- RSS feed URLs are requested from podcast publishers to load show and episode information.
- Audio media URLs are requested from podcast publishers to stream or download episodes.
- Cover artwork URLs are requested to display podcast artwork.
- Some legacy podcast publishers may provide RSS feeds or media URLs over HTTP. Echo Isle may access those URLs to support public podcast feeds that have not migrated to HTTPS.
- If a direct RSS request fails, Echo Isle may retry through an HTTPS proxy so that the feed can be loaded.

These third-party services and publisher servers may receive technical information such as your IP address, device network metadata, request time, search terms, RSS URLs, or media URLs as part of normal network operation.

## Third-Party Podcast Content

Podcast titles, artwork, descriptions, episode metadata, and audio URLs are provided by the respective podcast publishers through public RSS feeds or podcast search sources. Echo Isle does not host, modify, sell, or redistribute podcast content.

Downloaded episodes are stored locally inside the app for your personal offline listening.

## No Account Required

Echo Isle does not require you to create an account. The app does not collect a username, password, profile, payment account, or social account login.

## Analytics, Ads, And Tracking

Echo Isle does not include advertising SDKs. Echo Isle does not use your podcast subscriptions, playback progress, playback history, downloads, or RSS imports for advertising tracking.

If analytics, crash reporting, accounts, cloud sync, or other third-party services are added in a future version, this Privacy Policy will be updated before release.

## Children

Echo Isle is not directed to children. Podcast content is provided by third-party publishers and may vary by show, episode, country, region, and publisher.

## Your Choices

You can:

- Delete downloaded episodes from the app.
- Clear cache where the app provides cache controls.
- Remove subscriptions from the app.
- Delete the app to remove locally stored app data from your device.
- Contact support for privacy questions.

## Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app, legal requirements, or third-party services change. The updated policy will be posted on this website with a new effective date.
