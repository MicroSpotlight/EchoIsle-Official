# Podcast Content And Rights Policy

Echo Isle is a podcast client. It discovers, displays, streams, and downloads podcast content from publisher-provided public RSS feeds and podcast search sources.

## What Echo Isle Accesses

Echo Isle may access:

- Podcast titles.
- Podcast artwork.
- Show descriptions.
- Episode titles.
- Episode descriptions.
- Publication dates.
- Episode duration and metadata.
- Audio enclosure URLs.
- RSS feed URLs.

This information is provided by podcast publishers through public RSS feeds or podcast directory sources.

## What Echo Isle Does Not Do

Echo Isle does not:

- Host podcast audio on its own servers.
- Modify podcast audio.
- Sell third-party podcast content.
- Redistribute downloaded audio outside the app.
- Claim ownership of publisher content.

## Offline Downloads

Echo Isle allows users to save episodes locally inside the app for personal offline listening. Downloaded files remain app-managed local files and are not provided as a sharing or redistribution feature.

## Publisher Changes

If a publisher removes, changes, restricts, or disables an RSS feed or audio URL, Echo Isle may no longer be able to retrieve that content.

## Publisher Requests

If you are a podcast publisher and have a rights, attribution, removal, or feed handling request, please open an issue or contact the project through the support page:

- Support: https://echoisle.microspotlight.team/support
- GitHub Issues: https://github.com/MicroSpotlight/EchoIsle-Official/issues

Please include:

- Show title.
- RSS feed URL.
- Episode URL, if applicable.
- What action you are requesting.
- Information that helps verify your authority to make the request.
